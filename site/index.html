How to use

Browse menus for each dining hall using the tabs. Switch between meals with the toggle at the top.

Filter by diet

Tap filter buttons to show only matching items. Tap "More" for dietary restriction filters (Vegan, Vegetarian, Gluten Free, Halal, Kosher). You can combine multiple filters.

Labels

V VG GF H K
V = Vegan, VG = Vegetarian, GF = Gluten Free, H = Halal, K = Kosher
Low CO₂ High CO₂ Nutritious Spicy
Environmental and nutritional indicators from Michigan Dining.
Beef Pork Chicken Fish
Meat type detected from the item name.
Rare
Items that rarely appear on the menu (fewer than 2 times in the past 2 weeks). Don't miss these!

Allergens

Use the "Exclude" filters in the More panel to hide items containing specific allergens. Allergen data comes from Michigan Dining and may not cover all ingredients.

Nutrition

Hover (or tap) an item to see calories, protein, fat, and carbs in the popover.

About

Not affiliated with Michigan Dining. Menu data is scraped from the UMich dining website and may not always be accurate.
